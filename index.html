The Persimmon Tree Narrative

Mike Brookshire

Never has a book of interactions between a man on his back porch and a talking tree produced such laughter and joy. From the future of American football to the contemplation of old age and death, to the preparation of hurricane suppression systems, serious topics are made funny, and Mike learns about easier living from his supra-human mentor.

THE PERSIMMON TREE NARRATIVE combines fictional memoir and fictional self-improvement. It is an odd combination of improbable genre, but with Tree as a central character, the fictional nature of the narrative is difficult to overlook.
